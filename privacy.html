Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Hoosier Dirt & Gravel ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit hoosierdirtandgravel.com or contact us about our services. By using our website or reaching out to us, you agree to the practices described below.

Information We Collect

We only collect information that helps us respond to you and provide our services:

  • Information you give us — your name, phone number, email address, property address, and details about the work you're requesting, whether submitted through our contact form, by phone, by text, or by email.
  • Automatically collected information — basic technical and usage data such as your browser type, device, and pages visited, which may be gathered through cookies and similar technologies (see our Cookie Policy).

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and schedule free on-site estimates.
  • To prepare quotes, perform the work you've requested, and follow up on projects.
  • To process payments and, when requested, facilitate financing through third-party platforms.
  • To maintain and improve our website and services.

How We Share Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information only:

  • With service providers that help us operate, such as website hosting, form, financing (for example, the Joist platform), or scheduling tools — and only as needed to perform their work.
  • When required by law, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use cookies and analytics tools to understand how visitors use the site. You can control cookies through your browser settings. For details, see our Cookie Policy.

Data Retention

We keep your information only as long as needed to provide our services, maintain our business records, and meet legal obligations. When it's no longer needed, we take reasonable steps to delete or secure it.

Your Choices

You may request that we update or delete the personal information we hold about you, or ask us to stop contacting you, by reaching out using the details below. We'll honor reasonable requests as required by applicable law.

Children's Privacy

Our website and services are intended for adults and property owners. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Third-Party Links

Our site may link to third-party websites (for example, social media or financing partners). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
