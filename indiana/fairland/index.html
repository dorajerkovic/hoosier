Built For Fairland Ground

Fairland sits in northwest Shelby County near the I-74 corridor, surrounded by the open, flat farmland that defines this stretch of central Indiana. Flat ground drains slowly, so without the right grade and culvert sizing a driveway here turns muddy fast. We plan the water flow before the rock ever goes down so a Fairland drive sheds rain instead of holding it.
